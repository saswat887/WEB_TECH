Programming Language:-
It is a language through which we can provide instruction tp our device to get our desired output.

JavaScript:-
It is a high level,interpreted programming language who supports OOPS concepts.

High Level Language:-Language easily understood by human.

JS usages a software named interpreter to convert high level language to low level language thats why it is called interpreted.
Line by line compilation and execution is said to be interpretation.

Features:-
1.It is lightweight language
2.Interpreted language
3.Case Sensitive 
4.Platform Independent
5.Portable language
6.Supports OOPS concept 
7.Dynamically typed language
